Dobbies Shop Assistant

A website I created to replace the functionality of a till book at my current job at the garden center. The website allows for quick search of products via an autocomplete and dropdown select and presents a scan-able QR code which adds the product to the till. Reducing the time per transaction and thereby making me and my colleagues day to day tasks much faster and nicer.
