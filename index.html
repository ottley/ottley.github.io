Websites

Good For Paddleboarding?

 A sea, tide and weather condition website that collects data from an API, stores it in a database, analyses it and then displays it. Offering forecasts every three hours from 6AM to 6PM for today and the next two days after. Whenever available the most up to date forecasts are stored and prioritised. The site displays the data both in text as well as a visual gauge to give the answer to the question.. is it Good For Paddleboarding?

Simon Says Game

 A classic game I have recreated. Built to be responsive for different devices. Its simplistic style of play combined with being compatible with mobile has meant that I have played a fair bit of my own creation on the go. Hit start game and a coloured tile will be flashed, hit that tile and the next round will a add a new random tile until a mistake leads to a game over!

Dobbies Shop Assistant

 An assistant website that I created to replace the need for a till book at my current job at the garden center. The website allows for the quick search of products via autocomplete or selection from a dropdown. Once a product is selected it presents a scan-able QR code that adds the product to the till. This saves time each transaction by avoiding flicking through pages of barcodes and makes me and my colleagues workday more enjoyable.
